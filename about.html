About

Greentility is a free web application that allows users to organize all of their monthly expenses. Users can select what type of Bill they would like to add (e.g. gas, electric, water, etc.). Next, users can input the data of their individual bills with a month/year and a cost. After this, each bill in each specific bill category will be graphed to show utility fluctuation throughout the year.
